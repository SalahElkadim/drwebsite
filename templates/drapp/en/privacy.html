Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 05/04/2025

1. Introduction

At Sabr Consult, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our forensic linguistic consultation services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • When filling out consultation forms (e.g., name, email address, phone number).
  • Any documents or texts you send for forensic linguistic analysis.

Automatically Collected Data:

  • IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via cookies).

3. Legal Basis for Data Processing

  • Your explicit consent (when submitting a consultation request).
  • Compliance with legal obligations (when data is requested by judicial authorities).

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide specialized forensic linguistic consultation.
  • To enhance user experience and improve our website.
  • To contact you in urgent cases (e.g., detecting a legal threat in submitted texts).

5. Data Protection

  • We use SSL encryption technology to secure your data during transmission.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties unless:
    • We have your explicit consent.
    • Or if required by law (e.g., a court order).

6. Cookies

We use cookies for analytical purposes (e.g., Google Analytics). You can control or disable them through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. You will be notified via email or through an announcement on our website.
